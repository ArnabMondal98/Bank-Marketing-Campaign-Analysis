📊 Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis
Customer Conversion & Revenue Optimization using EDA
📌 Business Problem

A bank conducted a telemarketing campaign in 2017 to promote term deposits.
The objective was to identify:

High-potential customers

Key factors influencing term deposit subscription

Strategies to improve future campaign ROI

🎯 Project Objective

Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Identify conversion drivers

Derive actionable business insights

Recommend customer targeting strategy

📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:

Customer Attributes: Age, job, marital status, education, account balance, loan status

Campaign Attributes: Call duration, previous contacts, previous outcome, last contact date

Target Variable: Term Deposit Subscription (Yes/No)

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Python (Pandas, NumPy)

Matplotlib & Seaborn

Excel

SQL

Power BI

🧹 Data Cleaning Steps

Removed duplicates

Handled missing values

Standardized categorical variables

Treated outliers using IQR method

Created derived columns (e.g., age groups, campaign frequency categories)

📊 Exploratory Data Analysis
🔹 Univariate Analysis

Age distribution

Job category frequency

Balance distribution

Conversion rate

🔹 Bivariate Analysis

Call duration vs Subscription

Previous outcome vs Subscription

Loan status vs Subscription

Age group vs Conversion rate

🔎 Key Business Insights

✔ Call duration is the strongest predictor of conversion
✔ Customers with successful previous campaign outcomes have higher probability
✔ Certain job segments respond better
✔ Customers without housing loans show better subscription rates

💡 Business Recommendations

Target customers with positive previous outcomes

Increase engagement time in calls

Focus on specific age & job segments

Reduce cold calls to low-probability groups

📈 Business Impact

Improved targeting efficiency

Reduced marketing cost

Increased campaign conversion rate

Higher ROI
